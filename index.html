Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Arihant Dental Care.

Yes, absolutely. At Arihant Dental Care, we use the latest rotary endodontic techniques and effective local anaesthesia. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the procedure actually is — it feels no different from a routine filling. You can go back to work the same day.
Professional teeth whitening results can last anywhere from 1 to 3 years depending on your diet and oral hygiene habits. Avoiding tea, coffee, and tobacco helps maintain the brightness longer. We also offer touch-up kits for home use.
Braces can be applied at any age, but ideally treatment begins between ages 10–14 when the jaw is still developing. Adults can also benefit significantly from braces or clear aligners. Dr. Rohan will assess your case and recommend the best option.
Smile designing costs vary based on the number of teeth and procedures involved. At Arihant Dental Care, we provide a detailed cost estimate after your free consultation. We also offer flexible payment options to make premium care accessible for everyone.
Yes, we welcome walk-in patients, especially for dental emergencies. However, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure minimal waiting time and dedicated attention from Dr. Rohan. You can book online or WhatsApp us directly.
We follow strict hospital-grade sterilization protocols. All instruments are autoclaved before every use. Single-use disposables are used wherever required. The clinic environment is sanitized regularly, ensuring a completely safe experience for every patient.
